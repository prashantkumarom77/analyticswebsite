Who We Are

Built for Analysis, Not Execution

LLUBRAEB Analytics is a specialized financial technology firm headquartered in Hyderabad, dedicated exclusively to the aggregation, normalization, and delivery of Indian market data.

Built by a team that deeply understands the intricacies of market mechanics over our 6 years in operation, our infrastructure is engineered to eliminate the friction typically associated with historical backtesting and live market feeds. We are completely platform agnostic—we supply the raw intelligence, you execute on your own platforms.
