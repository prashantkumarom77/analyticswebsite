03 Accordion / FAQ

Do you provide tick-level historical data?
Yes. Our historical archives include granular tick-by-tick data, allowing for precise slippage modeling and high-frequency backtesting.
Are datasets adjusted for corporate actions?
Absolutely. All historical equity data is meticulously adjusted for corporate actions, including splits, bonuses, and dividends.
Can I execute trades directly through your API?
No. LLUBRAEB Analytics is strictly a data vendor. We provide the intelligence; you use your own connected brokerage to actually execute the trades.
Can my team test the data before purchasing?
Yes. We provide sample datasets so your developers and quants can evaluate our data structure and cleanliness before making a commitment.
How do I access the live market feeds?
You can connect via our robust REST APIs and WebSockets to receive normalized, clean, and structured feeds ready for immediate modeling.
Do you offer enterprise infrastructure setups?
Yes, we offer custom licensing, dedicated support, and bespoke feed configurations specifically designed for institutional desks.
